DreamCine — Support

Need help? We’re here for you. Use the options below to get support, report a problem, or manage your account.

Contact Us

• Email support: support@dreamcine.app
• Abuse reports: abuse@dreamcine.app
• Privacy inquiries: privacy@dreamcine.app

Response time: within 24 hours.

Report an Issue

When you email us, please include:

  • Your DreamCine username or the email used for login
  • Device & OS (e.g., iPad Pro 12.9, iPadOS 18.0)
  • App version (Profile → About → Version)
  • Short description + steps to reproduce
  • Screenshots or screen recording, if possible

Or use the in-app menu: Profile → Support → Report a Problem.

Account & Data

  • Delete account: In the app go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account. Your account and associated data will be permanently removed.
  • Privacy Policy: dreamcine.app/privacy.html
  • Terms of Service: dreamcine.app/terms.html

Subscriptions & Payments

  • Manage subscriptions in your App Store account settings.
  • Refunds are handled by Apple. Learn more in Apple’s support resources.

Safety & Moderation

  • DreamCine has a zero-tolerance policy for illegal or abusive content.
  • Use Report on any item or email abuse@dreamcine.app. Reports are reviewed within 24 hours.
  • You can block abusive users from their profile in the app.

FAQ (Quick Answers)

  • Background audio: Yes, you can keep listening to a video’s audio track while using other apps.
  • Non-personalized ads: We use AdMob with non-personalized ads and do not track users across apps or websites.
  • Minimum age: DreamCine is 18+.
