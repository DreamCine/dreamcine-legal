DreamCine — Support

If you have questions, feedback, or run into an issue, we’re here to help.

Contact Us

• Email support: support@dreamcine.app
• Abuse reports: abuse@dreamcine.app
• Privacy inquiries: privacy@dreamcine.app

Response time: within 24 hours (Mon–Fri).

Report a Problem

Please include the following in your message to help us resolve issues faster:

  • Your DreamCine username or login email
  • Device & OS (e.g., iPad Pro 12.9, iPadOS 18.x)
  • App version (Profile → About → Version)
  • Steps to reproduce + screenshots/screen recording (if possible)

You can also report content or users directly in the app via the Report action.

Account & Data

  • Delete account: In the app go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account. Your account and associated data will be permanently removed.
  • Privacy Policy: dreamcine.app/privacy.html
  • Terms of Service: dreamcine.app/terms.html

Subscriptions & Payments

  • Manage your subscription in your App Store account settings.
  • Refunds are handled by Apple per App Store policy.

Safety & Moderation

  • Zero-tolerance for illegal or abusive content.
  • Use Report on any item or email abuse@dreamcine.app.
  • Reports are reviewed within 24 hours. You can also block users in their profile.
